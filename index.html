Bugatti Centodieci

Centodieci (2020-present)

The Bugatti Centodieci is a tribute to the legendary EB110, with a modern twist on its design and performance.
Based on the Chiron, the car takes design cues from the EB 110 such as the five round air intakes which resemble a diamond and the wedge shaped design language. A small horse shoe radiator grille present below the headlamps further harks back to the EB 110. Narrow headlights with LED daytime running function and sharp grooves are used to create an aggressive appearance.
